# CEO Review — scaffolde.ai build plan

> `plan-ceo-review` lens: ambition · product sharpness · user value. Reviewer posture: would a pre-seed investor
> leave this front door feeling the category is inevitable and this is the team to back? 2026-06-22.

## Verdict
Structurally sound (audience-routed, evidence-first, credibility loud) but **not yet sharp enough to earn the raise.**
The plan optimizes the *site's* conversion without making the *company's* wedge and traction undeniable — and for a
pre-seed front door, that substance gap is the whole game. Aesthetic conveys inevitability; substance doesn't yet back it.

## Scoring (CEO lens)
- **Ambition: 7/10** — "the site is itself enrolled in the verification plane it sells" + "become the standard/notary for AI attestation" is genuinely ambitious and ownable. But it's under-amplified (buried as a "signature mechanism").
- **Product sharpness: 5/10** — "verification & audit plane across Anthropic/OpenAI/Google" is a *platform* claim, not a *wedge*. No named beachhead buyer. Traction "TBD."
- **User value (investor): 6/10** — strong credibility (founder, evidence), weak why-now + traction proof.

## Must-fix before this front door is investor-ready
1. **Name ONE wedge.** Force a beachhead — e.g. "independent attestation for regulated enterprises that must prove AI decisions to an auditor": one buyer, one compliance trigger, one workflow. Let the hero + /investors *imply that one buyer*, not "any AI." A narrow wedge that implies a broad future is what gets funded; a broad platform claim reads as unfocused. (sharpness)
2. **Promote the self-attesting page to the headline.** The page carrying its own real, clickable proof is the *demo of the category's inevitability* — not a flourish. Make it THE bet, visibly. (ambition)
3. **/investors must carry a why-now beat + ≥1 real traction proof.** Why-now = the regulatory/enterprise-governance wave (AI Act enforcement, governance budgets). Traction = a live attestation record, a design-partner LOI, or a pilot. "Traction TBD" is the gap that sinks pre-seed — if none exists, that's a *company* gap to close now, not a site problem to hide. (user value)
4. **Enforce /product = self-serve inspectable artifact, NOT "request a demo."** This is how the page answers the riskiest assumption (venture product vs consulting) *by showing product*. Audience-routing already demotes booking — good; now make /product open *as* a working artifact, or investors read services. (assumption)
5. **Address founder commitment on /investors.** Investors will ask "is Gary full-time?" State transition intent; don't leave it implicit. (assumption)

## Keep (already sharp)
Audience-routing that demotes booking to secondary · evidence-over-assertion · plain-language thesis above the clever
line · the instrument credibility character · the self-attesting mechanism (just elevate it).

## One-line CEO note
The design system makes Scaffolde *look* inevitable; the plan now needs the **named wedge + one real proof point +
self-serve product surface** to make it *be* investable. Those are company-substance gaps the prettiest site can't cover.
