How it works

An independent layer between your AI and the people who must trust it.

Scaffolde sits outside the model vendors — setting acceptance criteria before work begins, then checking, evidencing, and attesting the result.

1Criteria set up front. Acceptance criteria are defined before the AI does the work.
2Checked & evidenced. What the AI was asked, did, and produced is captured and verified.
3Independently attested. A party with no stake in the model's reputation signs the proof.
